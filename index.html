About Us

Andover Properties is a real estate investment firm which owns, operates, and develops commercial property throughout the U.S., with a focus on alternative asset classes including self storage, car wash, small bay industrial, and manufactured housing. We are one of the largest private self storage owners in the U.S.

Founded in 2003, we are a best-in-class, vertically integrated, owner-operator with expertise in acquisitions, development, capital markets, investment structuring, property management, and asset management.

We are a trusted partner for institutional capital including Ares, Blackstone, and TPG Angelo Gordon as well as family offices and individual investors.

We come to market with approximately six investments per year, providing investors with unique access to our institutional-quality investment pipeline which provides tax-advantaged cash flow, offers diversification, and achieves strong risk-adjusted returns.

INVESTMENT PHILOSOPHY

We are focused on select asset classes where our vertically integrated platform provides proprietary insights and real-time information to identify patterns and opportunities. Our long-term view allows us to be opportunistic across investment cycles and our disciplined investment framework ensures we adhere to our guiding principles in all market conditions. Our capital partners provide the flexibility to invest up and down the capital stack.

Become an Investor

Andover targets opportunities that have the following characteristics:

High growth and/or under-supplied markets
Capital structure issues
Operator inefficiencies
Under-leased and/or under -rented assets
Opportunity to invest capital accreditively through renovation, expansion, or ground up construction
